ManifestMojo Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

Last updated: July 5, 2026

ManifestMojo ("we," "our," or "us") is a daily manifestation companion with affirmations, journaling, goals, and AI features for iOS, developed by SudarshanTechLabs. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app does and does not collect, how it is used, and the choices you have. By installing or using ManifestMojo you agree to the practices described below.

Overview

ManifestMojo is designed to be private by default. Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate accounts, servers, or databases that hold your personal information, and we cannot see the content you create in the app.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require an account or ask for your name, email, or phone number.
  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers or track you across apps or websites.

Data You Create

Any content you enter into ManifestMojo is stored locally on your device using Apple’s standard on-device storage. It stays under your control. Deleting the app removes this local data. If you have iCloud backup enabled for the device, your data may be included in your own encrypted Apple backup, which is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Permissions

ManifestMojo does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or health data. If a future version adds a feature that needs a permission, iOS will ask you first and this policy will be updated.

Third-Party Services

ManifestMojo includes optional AI features. When, and only when, you use an AI feature, the text you provide for that feature (for example, the diary, journal, or mood entries you choose to summarize) is sent to Google’s Gemini API to generate a response. That text is processed by Google under the Google Privacy Policy. We do not send this data anywhere else, and no data is sent to Google unless you actively trigger an AI feature.

We use no advertising, no analytics, and no crash-reporting services.

Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored on your device and protected by iOS app sandboxing and the device security controls you enable (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). Because your data lives on your device, keeping your device secure is the best way to protect it.

Children’s Privacy

ManifestMojo is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date above.
