# ManifestMojo Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 5, 2026
_Last updated: July 5, 2026_
**Version:** 1.0.0

ManifestMojo ("we," "our," or "us") is a manifestation companion for iOS, developed by SudarshanTechLabs. ManifestMojo is a daily manifestation companion with affirmations, journaling, goals, and optional AI features. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app does and does not collect, how it is used, and the choices you have. By installing or using ManifestMojo you agree to the practices described below.

## Overview

ManifestMojo is designed to be **private by default**. Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate accounts, servers, or databases that hold your personal information, and we cannot see the content you create in the app.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- ManifestMojo does not collect location data.

### Account Information
- No account is required. We do not ask for your name, email, or phone number.
- ManifestMojo contains no in-app purchases or subscriptions, so no billing information is processed.

### Device Information
- Content you create in the app is stored locally on your device. We do not collect device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or analytics events.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide the features you use, entirely on your device.
- We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone.
- We do **not** use advertising identifiers or track you across apps or websites.

## Storage and Retention

### Data Storage
- Your content is stored locally using Apple's standard on-device storage.
- If you have iCloud backup enabled for your device, your app data may be included in your own encrypted Apple backup, governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### Data Retention
- Local data remains until you delete it in the app or uninstall the app.

### Data Security
- Data is protected by iOS app sandboxing and the device security controls you enable (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).
- Any network communication that does occur uses HTTPS/TLS.

## Data Sharing

### Third Parties
- Google Gemini API (only when you use an optional AI feature)

We do **not** share your data with:
- Data brokers
- Advertising networks
- Analytics or tracking providers
- Any third party unrelated to a feature you actively use

## Third-Party Services

ManifestMojo includes **optional** AI features. When — and only when — you use an AI feature, the text you provide for that feature (for example, the diary, journal, mood, or goal entries you choose to process) is sent to **Google's Gemini API** to generate a response. That text is processed by Google under the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). No data is sent to Google unless you actively trigger an AI feature.

ManifestMojo uses **no advertising, no analytics, and no crash-reporting services.**

Please review the relevant third-party privacy policy:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Permissions

- ManifestMojo does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or health data.
- If a future version adds a feature that needs a permission, iOS will prompt you first and this policy will be updated.

## Your Rights and Controls

### Data Access
- You can view, edit, or delete your content directly inside the app.

### GDPR Rights (EU Users)
Because ManifestMojo keeps your data on your device and we hold no copy, you already control it directly. If you are in the EU you retain the rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object to processing. Since we do not collect or store your data, exercising these rights is done on your own device.

### Permissions
- You can review or revoke permissions any time in **iOS Settings -> ManifestMojo**.
- Revoking a permission may disable the related optional feature.

## Children's Privacy

- ManifestMojo is **not intended for children under 13**.
- We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.
- Parents and guardians should supervise children's use of apps that store personal content.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. Changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Deletion

ManifestMojo does not require an account and stores your data only on your device.

#### Option 1: In-App Deletion
1. Open ManifestMojo
2. Delete individual entries or content where the app provides controls
3. Uninstalling the app removes all local app-private data

#### Option 2: Email Request
Send an email to **sunny.sudarshan@gmail.com** (or **support@sudarshantechlabs.com**)
- Subject: "Data Deletion Request - ManifestMojo"
- We will respond, typically within 48 hours

### What Gets Deleted
- All local app data stored by the app on your device

### Data Retention After Deletion
- **Immediate:** deleting content in-app removes it from your device
- **Uninstall:** removes all app-private local data

## About ManifestMojo

ManifestMojo is built with privacy in mind, using:
- Swift and SwiftUI
- On-device storage for your data
- Google Gemini for optional, user-initiated AI assistance

The app collects only what is needed for the features described in this policy, and keeps your data on your device.
